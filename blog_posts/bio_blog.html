A little about myself...

January 1, 2015

I was born and raised in Denver Colorado. I have been in construction all my life. I have never worked in a fast food joint, never been a waiter, bar tender, I have just built things. I still am going to build things it will just be on the computer. Hi, my name is Troy I am up and coming software developer. I am 45 years old and can’t wait to see where this adventure leads me. Before finding ACLTC I applied and was accepted into Devbootcamp, Chicago. I went through the 9 weeks of phase 0 and went through phase 1… twice! I was asked to leave the program. It was a experience that I will never forget. It was the hardest thing I have taken on in a long time. With that being said, their learning style just didn't fit my learning style. I feel that the style that Jay offers will fit me to a tie and I am excited to get started and meet and work with everyone of you!


I would love any and all feedback on github on any of my code. For those of you that don't know, look for the round circle with '!' with in the circle next to this circle it says 'issues' click on that. this can be found in the upper right hand side of the page. This will bring you to a screen that you can leave feedback for me. Make sure you explain what challenge your leaving feedback for and remember great feedback is kind, actionable, and specificity!
